What is Donation Station?

Through our site, you would now be able to search for thrift stores in your region and make a heartful move to give old garments to the destitute and the poor individuals. This is a stage that straightforwardly connects you to the shops that give away old garments to associations that help oppressed individuals. Little assistance from your side can help individuals wear garments, yet in addition a grin on their faces.

How to Donate?

Navigate to the search bar of our website and enter the place you are interested at donating. You can also choose your location directly, which will give straight searches for thrift shops around you.
